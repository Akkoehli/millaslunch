Having moved to London aged 21, Milla worked in the fashion industry for several years. An industry obsessed with every tiny detail of a woman's life, Milla became overwhelmed, feeling she was never going to be good enough.


Finally, after saving a bit of money, Milla travelled to Barcelona to follow her dreams of becoming a chef. Starting out in a small kitchen in her apartment that she shared with four others, and handing out free lunch to anyone that would take it, Milla's Lunch has grown into a well established, go to lunch, not just for plant based eaters but for everyone.

Making on avger 70-90 lunches a day between her an her co-chef, Milla's lunch is growing in an exciting young business, always putting their customers, themselves and the planet first.
